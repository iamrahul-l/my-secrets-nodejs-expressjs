Gamer Secrets

  • Always adjust your brightness settings to the max in dark games like horror or stealth-based titles. This can help you spot enemies hiding in shadows.
  • When playing multiplayer games, use a custom keyboard shortcut for frequently used actions or items. It can give you a split-second advantage.
  • Don’t skip in-game tutorials, even if you’re familiar with the genre. Developers often include unique mechanics that you might miss.
  • If you're gaming on a PC, close unnecessary background applications to free up RAM and improve performance.
  • Learn to play with multiple types of control schemes (keyboard, controller, mouse) so you can switch based on what gives you the best performance in a particular game.
  • In competitive games, watch replays of your losses. It helps you understand your mistakes and learn how to counter specific strategies in the future.
  • If your favorite game includes a day-night cycle, pay attention to how the environment and enemies change with time. Some quests or opportunities are only available during certain times.
